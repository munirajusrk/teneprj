CLIENT ACCOUNTS

Unique client-accounts are created in the system. Client admins can add users to the respective accounts.

TWO-LEVEL CODING

Clients can generate unique container and item codes so that every item is mapped to its container.

WITH OR WITHOUT SERIALISATION

Clients can choose to have unique QR codes for every item, or for a batch of items.

SAVE AND PRINT

Clients can save the codes generated. Codes can be exported for printing on pouches directly, or for printing on stickers. Clients can chose to export the codes in different sizes.

APIs FOR DATA TRANSFER

Clients, especially large industries, can chose to have the data transferred to their servers. We provide APIs for the same.

DASHBOARD

We provide dashboards that allow easy tracking of the QR codes generated and scanned.

SECURITY

The solution and data are securely maintained. The rights to the data belong to the respective clients entirely.
