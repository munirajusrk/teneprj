Sativus

A comprehensive platform for crop health management.

This white-labelled platform is designed to serve large clients, like state and national governments. It has been adopted by the Karnataka State Department of Agriculture in the name of eSAP (= Electronic Solutions against Agricultural Pests).

ENABLES CROP HEALTH SERVICES

The platform enables field users (extension agents) to independently provide crop health services to the farmers. Field users can register farmers, diagnose problems, estimate problem intensity, provide prescriptions, and follow-up.

CONTENT MANAGEMENT

Clients can manage the content — crops, problems, solutions — by themselves. The platform allows for location-specific content.

IDENTIFYING PROBLEMS

The platform generates a dichotomously branching, image-assisted architecture that guides field users to the problems. The architecture accommodates every crop and problem.

EXPERT SUPPORT

Clients can onboard their subject experts to the platform. The platform allows field users to connect with subject experts whenever such need arises.

ASSESSMENT OF PROBLEMS

The platform considers the type of problem, damage intensity, prevailing weather, expected market rates, etc., to assess the seriousness of a problem.

SOLUTIONS TO PROBLEMS

The platform allows field users to share the prescribed solutions with the farmers. Solutions are based not only on the crop and problem, but also on the location, crop age, and seriousness.

FOLLOW UP WITH FARMERS

The platform prompts field users to follow-up with the farmers. The follow up information is essential for improving/updating content.

HIGH QUALITY DATA

The platform has built-in mechanisms to prevent data duplication and false data. High quality data to the client is an important USP of the platform.

WORKS OFFLINE, UPDATES ONLINE

The field user app synchronises with the server when online and works in the field oﬄine. Services can be seamlessly provided even in remote landscapes.

LOCAL LANGUAGES

The platform enables clients to include content in local languages. Field users can choose the language they are familiar with.

FLEXIBLE HOSTING OPTIONS

The platform can be hosted on-premise, or on the client cloud. It can be branded and customised to suit the client. We continuously work with our clients to ensure successful deployment.
