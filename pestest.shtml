Pestest

A training cum evaluation system in Crop Health Management.

Insects, diseases and nutritional issues affect crop health. About 25% of a crop is lost despite spending about 25% of the total cost of cultivation. Additionally, improper crop health management is harming human health and the ecosystem. Early diagnosis is key to solving this concern. PesTesT aims to train extension agents, farmers, dealers, and students on a simple methodology for accurate diagnosis.

CAPACITY BUILDING IN CROP HEALTH DIAGNOSIS

The app has videos that help the stakeholders in describing the symptoms and guessing the causal groups.

CROP-CENTRIC APPROACH

Our methodology moves the stakeholders away from a narrow problem-centric approach to a holistic crop-centric approach.

UNIVERSAL LANGUAGE FOR DIAGNOSIS

The stakeholders are trained on a universal language for diagnosis. It allows them to handle any situation with confidence.

PRACTICE IN REAL FIELDS

PesTesT will soon have the Practice feature that allows trainees to apply the methods for diagnosing actual field problems.

DIFFERENT APPS USE OUR METHOD OF DIAGNOSIS

Our diagnosis methodology is simple and scalable. It is being adopted by different mobile apps like eSAP and the upcoming FarmaFy.

EVALUATING TRAINEES

Applications like eSAP (adopted by the Government of Karnataka) uses PesTesT to evaluate stakeholders before providing login access to extension agents.

CERTIFICATE AS PROOF OF SUCCESSFUL TRAINING

PesTesT generates certificates to the successful trainees on behalf of our partners who use it for training and evaluation.
