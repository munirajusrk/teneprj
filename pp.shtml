What Information do We Collect about You?

We collect some or all of the following information from you when you visit one of our apps.

  • Name (unverified, mandatory)
  • Photograph (unverified, non-mandatory)
  • Mobile Number (mandatory, verified through OTP)
  • State, District, Subdistrict (unverified, mandatory wherever asked)
  • Year of Birth (unverified, mandatory wherever asked)
  • Gender (unverified, mandatory wherever asked)
  • Highest Education (unverified, mandatory wherever asked)
  • Profession (unverified, mandatory wherever asked)

The mobile number is used to authenticate your login using One Time Password (OTP). It ensures that the content accessed by you remains discrete. Unverified information helps in providing you the right content through our apps.

We may also collect information from mobile devices that are necessary for providing our services:

  • Location (GPS): Location data, wherever collected, helps to create an accurate representation of the crop/business and its related parameters, which helps in bringing to you more targeted and relevant services
  • Camera (to capture pictures other than the earlier-mentioned ‘Photograph’): Granting camera permission allows the user to capture/upload pictures/videos straight from the app, which is used to validate the crop situations faced by the customer, or to understand the prevailing situations of the crops.
